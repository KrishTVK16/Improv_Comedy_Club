Improv 101: The Basics

Unlock your creativity and learn the art of spontaneous comedy.

Class Overview

This 8-week course is the perfect introduction to the world of improvisation. You will learn the core principles of "Yes, And," active listening, and supporting your scene partners. It's a fun, low-pressure environment designed to get you out of your head and into the moment.

Whether you want to perform on stage, improve your public speaking, or just meet new people, Improv 101 is the place to start.

What You'll Learn

Agreement ("Yes, And")
Character Creation
Object Work
Group Mind
